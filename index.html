Safe, serene environment and highly conducive to learning with impressively designed building with “TOMORROW” inside.

WHY SILVER STONE PUBLIC SCHOOL IS AMONGST THE BEST SCHOOL ?

State-of-the-Art Infrastructure – A benchmark of Quality and Excellence

Established in 2003 on land of 20 Bighas as an English medium Co-Educational School. The school has been approved by Central Board for sec Education (CBSE Affiliation No.2131060) due to excellent educational standards and infrastructures. The school is committed to the philosophy of an education system where the CHILD COMES FIRST. Academic excellence is the most important, however importance of sports, arts , crafts, dramatics, music and dance can not be understated.
